# Agora Ngo Crm
> Keep your connections moving.

## About The Project
In student NGOs, yearly mandate changes often lead to a massive loss of institutional memory. Contact handovers get messy, and the history of corporate interactions is frequently lost in scattered spreadsheets. 

Arcadia CRM is a specialized relationship management tool built specifically to solve this. It streamlines volunteer outreach, tracks the entire sponsorship journey, and preserves interaction history across generations of NGO members.

### The Core Problem Solved
When a new volunteer takes over corporate relations, they immediately know if a company was contacted last year, who they spoke to, and why a deal was accepted or rejected.

## Key Features
* **Global Context Switcher:** Select an active project (e.g., Job Fair 2026 or CariereInIT) from the master navigation. All actions, mass mails, and status updates are automatically recorded under that specific event.
* **Role-Based Access Control:** * `Externe CD`: Full read/write access to manage contacts, update statuses, and log history.
  * `General CD (President/Secretary)`: View-only access to monitor overall organisational progress.
* **Complex Firm Profiles:** A centralised hub for each company featuring:
  * A 7-step sponsorship journey tracker (from initial contact to promo delivery and payment).
  * Interaction history logs (emails, calls, meetings).
  * Specific organisational details (e.g., "No HQ in Romania", specific ticket sizes).
* **Toggleable Statistics (Silver Update):** Switch between a raw tabular view of all firms and a visual analytics dashboard showing pie charts and bar graphs of event performance.
